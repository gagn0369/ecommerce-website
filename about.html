The Citrus Smiles Story

Our orchards are located in Mohave Valley, Arizona. There, our staff takes great care of each and every tree, hand picks the fruit, and packages it for you. We ensure that each and every box that gets shipped is filled with nothing but top quality fruits.

We sell our products online and at local markets. We have even begun to extend our product line to include juices and essential oils made from our products!

If you have any questions, please contact us.
